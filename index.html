LogiSlate Nexus Instructions

Welcome! Follow these steps to maximize the tool:

LogiSlate will filter, organize and group your 3rd-party invoicing for you.

  • Step 1: Duplicate invoice numbers need to be removed, you will need to add these back manually once the file is created.
  • Step 2: Upload your file, a template is available but not necassary.
  • Step 3: 'Generate Processed File' will export your file into separate tabs based on Sending Store for deliveries and Receiving Store for IBTs.
  • Step 4: You will still need to review each tab for Long Distance deliveries/IBTs.
